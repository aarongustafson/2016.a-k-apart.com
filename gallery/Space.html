Space

by Tristan Strathearn with Jonathan Croughton

Space is a responsive mobile up exploration of our solar system. Each planet's size and distance from the sun is roughly scaled to their actual measurements based on current scientific knowledge. As you scroll towards the sun you'll learn an interesting fact about each planet. The initial page load is about ~9kb and the total is about ~58kb after lazy loading for the full experience - but only if your browser supports all required features.

View the source of this project on GitHub: https://github.com/10k-apart-space/Space

Bring me to light

by Marek Černák with David Mateašák

Application Bring Me to Light is a little challenge about connecting electric circuit. The goal of this app is according to title brings the light to the circle. Not only that app can perfectly connect up your brain activity into the scene also this app can you learn how electric circuits and their parts work. We have successfully built two levels of this app to 10kB and then we fell in love with this idea so we created an website and we decided to continue to develop next levels.

View the source of this project on GitHub: https://github.com/BringMeToLight/project
